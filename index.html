Move to Zero

Move to Zero is Nike's journey toward zero carbon and zero waste, helping to protect the future of sport.

What We're Doing:

  • RECYCLED MATERIALS
    78% of products use recycled content
  • NIKE GRIND
    Turns waste into courts, tracks, and gear
  • CLEAN ENERGY
    77% of operations powered by renewables
  • CIRCULAR DESIGN
    Programs like Nike Refurbished and Reuse-A-Shoe
We're transforming waste into products. Most of our footwear and apparel now feature materials that began life as something else: plastic bottles, old shoes, leftover textiles. What once ended up in landfills is now part of high-performance gear, reimagined through innovative design. Across our global operations, we're making a clean energy shift, with the majority already powered by renewables. And when products reach the end of their journey, we give them a second life‚Äîrepairing, refurbishing, or repurposing them into everything from running tracks to playgrounds. This is the future we're building: circular, sustainable, and efficient.

About Nike

Nike began in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, founded by track coach Bill Bowerman and runner Phil Knight. In 1971, they renamed it Nike‚Äîafter the Greek goddess of victory‚Äîand introduced the Swoosh logo. From creating the innovative waffle sole to launching the legendary Air Jordan line, Nike quickly became a leader in sportswear. Fueled by athlete partnerships and cutting-edge designs, Nike grew into a global brand known for performance, innovation, and inspiration, with over 1,000+ stores across 170 countries covering retail, online, and wholesale distribution.

Our Journey

What started as a simple idea in a garage has evolved into a movement that transcends sport. Nike's journey is one of relentless innovation, from Bill Bowerman's first waffle sole experiment to today's cutting-edge performance technologies. We've partnered with the world's greatest athletes, from Michael Jordan to Serena Williams, not just to create products, but to inspire generations. Every swoosh represents a story of determination, every shoe a step toward greatness. We believe that if you have a body, you are an athlete, and our mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world.
